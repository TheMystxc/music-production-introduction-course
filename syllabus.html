Music Production

Instructor: Colwyn Hoard

Contact: choard@thegilmore.org

Duration: 15 Weeks

Please select a specific session from the Homepage to view dates and times.

This course explores the creative and technical fundamentals of music production. Students will learn how to deconstruct the music they listen to and rebuild it using modern tools. The course begins with building loops in BandLab to master song structure and texture, then transitions to Logic Pro to explore advanced MIDI, synthesis, and audio effects.

Unit 1: Texture, Loops, & BandLab

Week 1: Foundations & First Steps

Topics What is Music?; Linear vs. Layers; Structure vs. Texture; Juxtaposition vs. Superimposition.

Activities Lab Expectations; Setting up BandLab accounts; Personal Info Form.

Project Start Loops and Layers Project (BandLab).

Week 2: Listening & Layout

Topics Refresher on Song Layers; Talking about Music (Language & Terminology).

DAW Skills Intro to DAWs; Tutorial on Loops (Moving, Volume, Looping).

Activities Song Recreation Activity; Group Listening Party (Discussion).

Project Continue Loops and Layers Project.

Week 3: Rhythm & Studio Flow

Topics Drum Patterns & Sequencing.

DAW Skills Continued DAW Tutorial (Loop focus).

Activities Studio Time Focus.

Project Continue Loops and Layers Project.

Week 4: Polishing the Mix

Topics Tips & Tricks (Fading, Panning, Balance); Automation (Volume/Pan); Clip Editing (Speed, Pitch Shift, Reverse, Cutting); Making satisfying transitions.

Activities Studio Time Focus.

Deadline Finish Loops and Layers Project.

Unit 2: Theory & Transition to Logic Pro

Week 5: MIDI Basics

DAW Skills Introduction to MIDI in BandLab (Instruments, Recording, Editing).

Activities Group Listening: Genre Loop Projects.

Project Group Listening Party (Discussion).

Week 6: Music Theory & Logic Pro

Theory Scales (Major/Minor); What is a Melody?; Harmonic Progressions (Basic Chord Theory).

DAW Skills Introduction to Logic Pro; Basic Logic Tutorial.

Activities Harmony Demo; Progression Demo; Group Keyboard Activity.

Week 7: Logic Pro Deep Dive

DAW Skills MIDI in Logic Pro (Virtual Instruments, Recording, Editing).

Activities Exploration Time (MIDI).

Unit 3: The MIDI + Loop Project

Week 8: Instrumentation & Structure

Topics Song Layers related to Instruments; Song Structure (Generic vs. Specific, Revisit Layers vs. Linear).

Project Start MIDI + Loop Project.

Week 9: Production Hygiene & Recording

Topics Things to avoid (Clipping, Over-density, Frequency masking); Introduction to Recording Audio.

Project Continue MIDI + Loop Project (Recording assistance available).

Weeks 10-11: Audio Effects & Mixing

Topics Time-based (Delay, Reverb); Modulation (Chorus, Flanger); Dynamics (Compression); Spectral (EQ, Filter).

Project Continue MIDI + Loop Project (Recording assistance available).

Weeks 12-13: Mixing, Mastering, & Advanced Processing

Topics Chaining Effects; Automation of Effects parameters.

Project Continue MIDI + Loop Project.

Deadline Optional Early Turn-in.

Unit 4: Finalization

Week 14: Studio Lab

Activities Assist Student Projects; Final Polish.

Deadline Final Turn-in: MIDI + Loop Project.

Week 15: Conclusion

Activities Final Listening Party and Discussion; Exporting projects off iMacs. Final Free Studio Time.
