Course Lessons

How to navigate this page: Below is the full directory of weekly modules for the course, organized by unit. Click on any week's title to open a dedicated page containing your reading materials, embedded video tutorials, interactive examples, and step-by-step project instructions for that session.

Unit 1: Texture, Loops, & BandLab

Week 1: Foundations & First Steps

An introduction to course concepts like linear vs. layered composition, setting up your BandLab account, and starting the Loops and Layers project.

Week 2: Listening & Layout

A refresher on song layers, basic DAW tutorials covering loops and volume, and group listening discussions to build our musical vocabulary.

Week 3: Rhythm & Studio Flow

Focusing on drum patterns and sequencing, with continued DAW tutorials and dedicated studio time to build your track.

Week 4: Polishing the Mix

Tips and tricks for fading, panning, clip editing, and finalizing your Loops and Layers project before the deadline.

Unit 2: Theory & Transition to Logic Pro

Week 5: MIDI Basics

An introduction to MIDI instruments, recording, and editing within BandLab.

Week 6: Music Theory & Logic Pro

Exploring scales, melodies, and basic chord theory, paired with an introduction to the Logic Pro interface.

Week 7: Logic Pro Deep Dive

Advanced MIDI techniques in Logic Pro, including virtual instruments and precise MIDI editing.

Unit 3: The MIDI + Loop Project

Week 8: Instrumentation & Structure

Applying song layers to specific instruments, exploring song structure, and starting the MIDI + Loop Project.

Week 9: Production Hygiene & Recording

Best practices to avoid clipping and frequency masking, plus an introduction to recording live audio.

Weeks 10-11: Audio Effects & Mixing

Exploring time-based, modulation, dynamics, and spectral effects (Reverb, Delay, EQ, and Compression).

Weeks 12-13: Mixing, Mastering, & Advanced Processing

Advanced techniques for effect chaining and automation to finalize the mix.

Unit 4: Finalization

Week 14: Studio Lab

Dedicated studio time for project assistance and final polish ahead of the final turn-in.

Week 15: Conclusion

Final listening party, exporting projects, and a course wrap-up discussion.
